Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a receipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan using medium flame
  2. Gentaly mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once the butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20seconds(as if you are making an omlet)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the hear(it will continue to cook on the plate until it serve)
